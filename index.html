What is CTF?

Capture the Flag (CTF) in computer security is an exercise where participants attempt to find text strings, called "flags", which are secretly hidden in vulnerable programs or websites. CTFs can be **competitive or educational** and come in two main styles:

  • Jeopardy-style: Solve challenges to find flags from organizers.
  • Attack/Defense-style: Steal flags from other participants.

CTFs help develop **cybersecurity skills** and are used in both **academic and professional** settings.
